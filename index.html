FAQ

Questions, answered

How long does a typical project take? +
Starter projects take 2–3 weeks. Studio-tier work runs 6–10 weeks depending on scope. We always agree on a timeline during discovery before any money changes hands.
Do you work with international clients? +
Absolutely. We've delivered projects across 14 countries. All collaboration happens asynchronously via Notion, Figma, and Loom — timezone differences have never slowed us down.
Can I update the site myself after launch? +
Yes. Every project includes a CMS integration (Sanity, Contentful, or Notion as a backend) so you can edit content without touching code. We also provide a handoff video walkthrough.
What makes your animations different from a template? +
Templates animate the same thing for every client. We write custom shaders and physics systems from scratch, tuned to your brand's personality — the speed, density, colour, and behaviour are all deliberate choices made for you.
Do the animations affect page load speed? +
No. We ship animations as lightweight JS modules with lazy loading and reduced-motion fallbacks. Our sites consistently score 90+ on Google PageSpeed even with heavy visual effects.
